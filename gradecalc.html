Grade Calculator

Welcome to the Grade Calculator!
Please note, this calculator is specific to Enloe High School
You must save your grade before adding another one, however, you may edit your grade once it is entered
When entering your grade please insert the numerator of your grade in the left box and the denominator in the right box
Checking the "AP" checkbox will convert your Major and Minor category weightings appropriately
Your overall grade will update every time you click "save" in the grade dialog
The "lowest possible" field will display the lowest possible grade you could get on the current assignment and still recieve an A
Please do not enter Major/Minor grades and Quarter/Final Grades at the same time
